Common questions

Is this a replacement for a licensed estimator?

No. Outputs are draft quantities and planning notes for your qualified team to review. This is not a licensed estimate, bid, quantity survey, or building-code determination.

What trades or verticals are supported?

The assist scaffold adapts to your business description. It works best for planning-heavy verticals where first-pass drafts save significant time — construction, electrical, HVAC, landscaping, and similar.

What if I don't need the vertical assist?

Uncheck the vertical tool toggle in the builder and you get a standard Professional build. The assist is always opt-in.
