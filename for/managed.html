Common questions

Do I need a paid build first?

Yes — Managed Runtime is an add-on after any paid build tier (Foundation, Professional, or Enterprise). It keeps what was deployed running and monitored.

What happens when something breaks?

Automated health checks detect failures. The system retries where safe, logs everything, and flags issues that need your attention in the runtime report.

Can I cancel the managed subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime. Your deployed product continues running on your own accounts — you just lose the automated monitoring, retry, and optimization cadence.
