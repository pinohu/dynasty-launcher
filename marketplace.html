Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is Your Deputy?
Your Deputy is the launcher for autonomous business units. It generates and validates the website, funnel, products, CRM logic, RevOps, contracts, invoices, payments, onboarding, support, analytics, AI agents, MCP tools, event workflows, provider abstractions, self-hostable infrastructure, standards evidence, observability, deployment protection, and semantic promise-equivalence checks.
Which businesses does it work with?
The build contract is niche-driven. Each generated business must stay aligned to its target customer, location, business model, primary offer, secondary offers, pricing, content, proposals, contracts, onboarding, support, analytics labels, prompts, and tools.
How much does it cost?
Plans map to operating scope, not a website template count. The generated business unit can include revenue products, CRM logic, RevOps documents, checkout, payment abstraction, onboarding, support, analytics, AI agents, MCP tools, workflows, and self-hosted infrastructure. Optional vendor integrations depend on tier, configured keys, and provider health.
What tools does it integrate with?
The default architecture uses internally controlled provider abstractions for payments, email, storage, analytics, documents, signatures, CRM, notifications, and AI. Stripe, SendGrid, S3, and similar services are supported as optional adapters, but core business logic should not depend directly on any one vendor.
How long does setup take?
The builder generates the repo, repairs known failure classes, validates the contract, and records machine-readable degraded fallbacks when external credentials are missing. Self-hosted scaffolds continue without manual handoff, while real third-party activation waits for customer-owned credentials.
Can I try it before I pay?
Yes. Start with scoring and planning, then progress to a generated business unit when you want code, deployment, RevOps, products, checkout, onboarding, AI tools, and infrastructure. The maturity page shows what is live today, partial, code-generated, or planned.
Is my data safe?
Generated builds include an OWASP ASVS/SAMM-aligned checklist, JWT/RBAC, audit logging, input validation, tenant isolation, secure headers, safe webhook verification, and AI prompt-injection precautions. Formal certifications, penetration tests, and attorney/security reviews remain customer-owned readiness steps rather than hidden claims.
What if I need to change my plan?
You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. There are no long-term contracts, no cancellation fees, and no penalties. If you outgrow your current plan, we'll help you find the right tier.
