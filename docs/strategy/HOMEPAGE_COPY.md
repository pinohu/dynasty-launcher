## Tone and word choice

- **Yes:** plainspoken, outcome-first, practical, time-respecting
- **No:** "revolutionize", "transform", "unlock", "empower", "leverage", "seamless", "robust"
- **Say:** "add," "turn on," "catch," "recover," "keep," "close the gap"
- **Avoid naming Claude/AI/LLM in customer copy.** The value is the outcome, not the mechanism.
