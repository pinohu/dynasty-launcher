## CATEGORY 15: Invoicing & Billing

### 15.01 — Auto-Generate Invoice on Service Completion
**Personas:** Solo Steve, Manager Maria, Bookkeeper Beth
**Pain Point:** Project finishes on Tuesday. Invoice goes out the following Monday — or later — because someone has to manually create it.
**Cost of Inaction:** Every day an invoice is delayed is a day you're not getting paid. Average payment delay from late invoicing: 12 additional days. Cash flow suffers.
**AIDA:**
- **A:** "Your average invoice goes out 6 days after service completion. That's $8,000/month in delayed cash flow."
- **I:** "Project marked complete → invoice auto-generates with line items, pricing, and payment link → emails to client instantly."
- **D:** "Service ends at 3 PM. Invoice lands in client's inbox at 3:05 PM. Payment received by Thursday. Cash flow accelerated by 2 weeks."
- **Action:** "Connect project completion to invoicing. Templates included. $24/mo."

### 15.02 — Recurring Invoice Generator
**Personas:** Owner Omar, Bookkeeper Beth, Agency Alex
**Pain Point:** Monthly retainer invoices for 20 clients require manual creation each billing cycle. Someone forgets 3 clients. Those clients aren't billed for a month.
**Cost of Inaction:** Missed billing cycles = lost revenue. Manual process doesn't scale. Inconsistent billing dates confuse clients.
**AIDA:**
- **A:** "You forgot to invoice 3 retainer clients last month. That's $4,500 in delayed revenue you almost didn't collect."
- **I:** "Set billing day and amount per client. Invoices auto-generate and send every cycle with payment links included."
- **D:** "1st of every month: 20 invoices go out at 8 AM. Zero manual effort. Zero missed clients. Payment starts arriving by the 3rd."
- **Action:** "Set up recurring billing for each client. First cycle automated. $19/mo."
