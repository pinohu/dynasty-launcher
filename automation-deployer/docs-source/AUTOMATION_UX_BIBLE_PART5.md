## CATEGORY 21: Reputation & Review Management

### 21.01 — Post-Service Review Request
**Personas:** Solo Steve, Owner Omar, Marketing Mike | **Pain:** Happy clients don't leave reviews because nobody asks. Your 4.2 rating stays low while competitors who ask systematically climb to 4.8. | **Cost:** Every month without review requests is a month your Google ranking stagnates. Competitors with more reviews win the map pack.
**AIDA:** **A:** "Your competitor has 340 reviews. You have 47. Same quality service. They ask. You don't." **I:** "Automated review request email + SMS X days after service completion — with direct links to Google and Yelp." **D:** "Service completed Tuesday. Thursday at 10 AM: 'We loved working with you! Would you share your experience? [Google link] [Yelp link].' 25% leave a review. Your count doubles in 3 months." **Action:** "$14/mo."

### 21.02 — Positive Review Thank-You Responder
**Personas:** Solo Steve, Owner Omar | **Pain:** Positive reviews go unacknowledged. The reviewer feels ignored. Google's algorithm favors businesses that respond to reviews. | **Cost:** Unresponded reviews signal indifference to Google and potential customers.
**AIDA:** **A:** "15 five-star reviews without responses. Google notices. We're thrilled you had a great experience. We look forward to serving you again!' Posted automatically. Google rewards engagement. Potential clients see a business that cares." **Action:** "$19/mo."

Includes the review content and an AI-drafted response suggestion." **D:** "Alert at 10:02 AM — 2 minutes after the review posts. AI suggests a professional, empathetic response.
