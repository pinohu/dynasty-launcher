Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026 | Dynasty Empire LLC

1. Information We Collect

When you use Dynasty Launcher, we collect:

  • Account information: Email address and name via Clerk authentication.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
  • Build data: Project descriptions, configuration preferences, and generated file metadata stored in Neon PostgreSQL.
  • Usage analytics: Page views and feature usage via PostHog (anonymized).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provision your business services (GitHub repos, Vercel deployments, Stripe products, 20i hosting, etc.)
  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription tier
  • To improve the product and fix bugs
  • To send transactional emails related to your builds

3. Third-Party Services

Dynasty Launcher integrates with the following services on your behalf. Each service has its own privacy policy:

  • Stripe (payments) — stripe.com/privacy
  • Clerk (authentication) — clerk.com/privacy
  • Vercel (hosting) — vercel.com/privacy
  • GitHub (code repository) — github.com/privacy
  • Neon (database) — neon.tech/privacy
  • PostHog (analytics) — posthog.com/privacy
  • 20i (hosting/email) — 20i.com/privacy
  • Acumbamail (email marketing) — acumbamail.com/privacy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in:

  • Neon PostgreSQL (build history, license tracking) — encrypted at rest
  • Vercel (environment variables) — encrypted
  • Your browser localStorage (preferences, session data)

All API keys are stored in encrypted Vercel environment variables and never exposed to the browser.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your build history
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

Contact us at ikeohu@dynastyempire.com to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use PostHog for product analytics. PostHog uses cookies to track anonymous usage patterns. You can opt out by blocking cookies or using a privacy browser extension.

7. Data Retention

Build history is retained for 12 months. Account data is retained until you request deletion. Payment records are retained per Stripe's policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
