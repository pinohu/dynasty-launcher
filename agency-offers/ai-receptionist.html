Agent delivery workflow

  1. Extract service, pricing, location, hours, and booking rules.
  2. Build the voice persona and objection-handling tree.
  3. Connect Twilio, voice agent, calendar, and CRM logging.
  4. Run simulated calls for after-hours, spam, urgent, and booking cases.
