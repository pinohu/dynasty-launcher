Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is Your Deputy?
Your Deputy is a zero-touch automation platform built for service businesses. It provides 353 ready-to-use workflows that handle lead response, appointment scheduling, invoicing, review requests, and more — all running 24/7 without manual intervention. Think of it as a tireless digital employee that never misses a lead.
Which service businesses does it work with?
Your Deputy is built for plumbers, HVAC technicians, electricians, cleaners, pest control operators, and other field service businesses. Industry-specific editions come pre-configured with the exact workflows each trade needs — no technical setup required.
How much does it cost?
Individual modules start at $19/month each. Outcome packs bundle related workflows at a discount. Industry editions start at $149/month and include everything a specific trade needs. Every plan includes a 30-day money-back guarantee, and you can cancel anytime — no contracts, no hidden fees.
What tools does it integrate with?
Your Deputy connects with 100+ tools including Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, Google Calendar, Microsoft 365, Stripe, QuickBooks, Google Business Profile, Twilio, HubSpot, and Zapier. If you already use it, we probably already connect to it.
How long does setup take?
Most workflows activate in minutes with one-click setup. Connect your existing tools, pick the workflows you need, and Your Deputy starts working immediately. If you'd rather not do it yourself, our expert setup team can handle everything for a one-time fee.
Can I try it before I pay?
Yes. Browse the full catalog with no credit card required. When you're ready to activate, every paid plan includes a 30-day money-back guarantee — if it doesn't work for your business, you get a full refund, no questions asked.
