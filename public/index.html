FAQ

Straight answers

What strategy documents do I receive?

You receive 90+ artifacts spanning strategy, financial models, go-to-market, legal templates, and operational playbooks. See the full inventory.

Can I use these documents for funding applications?

Yes — the bundle is built to support fundraising and lending conversations with serious structure (plans, numbers, narrative, diligence-friendly organization). Treat them as a strong starting point: have your attorney, CPA, and any industry advisors review before you sign, file, or submit.

What live infrastructure is provisioned?

Foundation ($1,997) delivers documents, live product, Day-1 Kit, and the same 11-slot orchestrator allowlist as Professional when keys allow (see BUILD-MANIFEST.json). Professional ($4,997) is the higher build tier with that same allowlist. Enterprise ($9,997) expands to 13 slots (WordPress + post-deploy verification). Voice/SMS/CRM/directory need Custom Volume. Read /maturity before purchase.

How does the multi-model viability analysis work?

Multiple independent evaluations score your idea across several business lenses, then reconcile into one clear verdict. If the story doesn’t hold together, that shows up — before you spend. A final independent stress test helps surface weak points before they cost you money.

What is the ownership model?

You own what we deliver in your repository: product code, documents, and configuration. What’s “live” in third-party dashboards depends on what you activate using the guides in your bundle. Your bundle includes activation guidance; use what’s included to see typical readiness by category. Legal and regulated use still requires your qualified advisors.

What input methods are supported?

Nine input paths: type, speak, paste a URL, upload an image or document, record a video, use your camera, have a guided interview, or start from a template.

Do you support non‑US businesses or enterprise procurement?

Defaults (legal copy, entity examples, some vendor regions) are US-oriented. Non‑US and regulated industries need advisor review. Enterprise procurement (SSO, MSA, custom DPA) is not self-serve in the builder — contact sales for vendor review workflows.

What’s the difference between Strategy Pack and Foundation?

Strategy Pack ($697) delivers all 90+ documents as a downloadable zip — no code, no deployment. Foundation ($1,997) adds production code, deployment, and Day-1 Kit. Upgrade credit applies within 30 days.
