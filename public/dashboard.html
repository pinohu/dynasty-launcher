Frequently asked questions about the dashboard

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How do I access the dashboard?
Active subscribers can access the dashboard after logging in. If you're new, browse the marketplace to find the right plan for your service business. Setup takes minutes.
What can I manage from the dashboard?
Activate and pause workflows, monitor performance KPIs, review opportunity cards that suggest which automation to turn on next, manage capabilities, and track recent events.
How do I activate a new workflow?
From the dashboard, browse available modules and click Activate. Most workflows start running within minutes. You can also upgrade your plan to unlock additional modules from the marketplace.
Can I pause or cancel automations?
Yes. Pause any workflow instantly from the dashboard with one click. No penalties, no cancellation fees. Reactivate anytime you're ready.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your Deputy uses 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest. We never share or sell your customer data. All integrations use secure OAuth connections.
Who do I contact for help?
Email support@yourdeputy.com or visit the help center. Active subscribers get priority support with response times under 4 hours.
