Failed Payment Recovery

Automatically retry failed card charges, notify the customer, and capture updated payment methods.

$19/mo per client

Available in: professional, enterprise

Business Outcome

Recover involuntary churn from expired or declined cards.

How It Works

Trigger: payment.failed

Actions performed:

  1. retry schedule
  2. send update card link
  3. notify owner
  4. pause service if applicable

Configurable Settings

  • Retry schedule (days) — string, default: 1,3,5,7
  • Pause service after N days — int, default: 14
  • Grace amount (USD) — float, default: 5

Integrations Required

payments, invoicing, crm, email

Personas: office manager, owner operator
