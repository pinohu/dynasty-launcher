Estimate Follow-Up Sequence

After an estimate is sent, follow up on a 24h / 72h / 7d cadence until it's signed, declined, or expires.

$19/mo per client

Available in: professional, enterprise

Business Outcome

Convert more estimates into booked jobs without the owner chasing them.

How It Works

Trigger: estimate.sent

Actions performed:

  1. send email
  2. send sms
  3. create task
  4. move pipeline stage

Configurable Settings

  • Follow-up cadence — enum, default: 24h/72h/7d
  • Custom cadence (comma-separated hours) — string
  • Stop sending after estimate is viewed — bool, default: false
  • Also send SMS follow-ups — bool, default: true

Integrations Required

estimates, crm, email

Recommended For

Industries: Plumbing Service Business, HVAC Service Business, Electrical Contractor, Home Remodeling / Design-Build, Pest Control

Personas: owner operator, office manager, sales coordinator

Key Metrics

  • estimate close rate
  • time to decision
  • follow up reply rate
