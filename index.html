How we validate your idea
Before we build anything, we pressure-test the business.
You get a viability score backed by multiple analytical frameworks and stress-tested by an adversarial model — so you know exactly where you stand before investing further.
🔬

Scored across 8 frameworks

Your business is analyzed through Blue Ocean Strategy, Porter's 5 Forces, Hamilton Helmer's 7 Powers, Jobs-to-be-Done, Lean Canvas, market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM), Unit Economics, and Competitive Positioning. You see exactly where your idea is strong and where it's vulnerable.

🤖

Multiple perspectives, one score

Up to 14 AI models independently evaluate your business — then vote. The weighted consensus prevents any single model's blind spots from skewing the result. If they disagree, they deliberate until consensus emerges. You get a composite score you can defend to investors.

😈

Stress-tested before you are

After the consensus forms, an adversarial model attacks your strategy — finding weaknesses, hidden assumptions, and market risks. The critique is included in your deliverables. Better to discover these in a document than in a pitch meeting.

Frequently asked
Technical and commercial questions

What strategy documents are generated?

Nine documents totaling 30,000-50,000 words: BUSINESS-SYSTEM.md (McKinsey-format strategic architecture), REVENUE-MODEL.md (12-month financial projections with unit economics), GTM-PLAYBOOK.md (90-day launch plan), INVESTOR-READINESS.md (VC due diligence, SBA loan eligibility, Shark Tank readiness, IDEO framework, M&A exit package — all in one document), SBA-BUSINESS-PLAN.md (full SBA-format with 3-year financials), PITCH-DECK.md (10-slide investor pitch), AGENT-SYSTEM.md (AI architecture), FAILURE-MODES.md (10 scenarios with runbooks), and COMPETITIVE-ANALYSIS.md (real competitor research). Generated by Claude Sonnet 4 with 8-framework analysis and 14-model consensus scoring.

Can I use these documents for funding applications?

Yes. The INVESTOR-READINESS.md addresses all 10 criteria VCs evaluate, all 10 SBA loan requirements, and all 10 elements Shark Tank investors assess. The SBA-BUSINESS-PLAN.md follows the exact format SBA loan officers require — including 3-year P&L, monthly cash flow projections, break-even analysis, and funding request with use-of-proceeds breakdown. The M&A section covers everything an acquirer needs: EBITDA, valuation multiples, customer concentration, IP portfolio, and integration considerations. These documents are generated by frontier AI models (Claude Sonnet 4) and should be reviewed by your advisors before submission.

What live infrastructure is provisioned?

Depending on tier: deployed production application on custom domain with SSL, authenticated business email (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), Stripe Connected Account accepting real payments, Clerk authentication with isolated user organization, CRM records, AI voice agent, appointment scheduling, email marketing with 5-campaign sequence, SMS templates, FAQ chatbot, PostHog analytics, lead tracking, 7 automation workflows (signup, booking, payment, lead, missed call, dunning, review), legal document PDFs, and 260-post social media calendar. Each service is provisioned via live API calls — not mockups.

How does the multi-model viability analysis work?

Up to 14 AI models (Claude Sonnet 4, GPT-4o, Gemini 2.5 Pro, DeepSeek V3, Mistral Large, Llama 3.3 70B, and more) analyze your business across 8 frameworks simultaneously. Each model works independently — preventing groupthink. A weighted consensus (frontier models count more) determines the composite viability score. If consensus falls below 60%, models deliberate in additional rounds. A Devil's Advocate model then attacks the winning strategy ruthlessly. The result survives adversarial stress-testing and is defensible in investor conversations.

What is the ownership model?

You own all code (private GitHub repository), all documents (pushed to your repo), and all generated content. Stripe billing operates through a Connected Account — you receive your own dashboard and full control over payments. Clerk authentication uses a managed instance with an isolated organization for your users. Infrastructure runs on your domain. Credentials for every provisioned service are delivered in CREDENTIALS.md. OPERATIONS.md includes a complete business formation checklist with referrals to Dynasty Empire ecosystem partners for LLC formation, notarization, automation, and lead generation.

What input methods are supported?

Nine ways to describe your venture: type a description, speak via voice, paste a competitor URL for analysis, upload or photograph an image (business card, whiteboard, storefront), open your camera for live capture, record a video walkthrough, upload a document (pitch deck, business plan, research PDF), have a conversational interview with AI, or start from a template. All inputs are processed by Claude Vision and language models to extract a structured business brief.
