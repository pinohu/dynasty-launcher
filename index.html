FAQ

Straight answers

What strategy documents do I receive?

You receive 90+ artifacts (typically about 30,000–50,000 words of core material) spanning strategy, revenue and operating model, go-to-market, investor- and lender-ready packs, competitive landscape, pitch narrative, risk and failure-mode runbooks, product specification, data and API design, design system, content outlines, and customer discovery — organized in your bundle so your team (and your advisors) can work from one place. See the full file-by-file inventory.

Can I use these documents for funding applications?

Yes — the bundle is built to support fundraising and lending conversations with serious structure (plans, numbers, narrative, diligence-friendly organization). Treat them as a strong starting point: have your attorney, CPA, and any industry advisors review before you sign, file, or submit.

What live infrastructure is provisioned?

Foundation delivers your document set + live product + deployment, plus a Day-1 Success Kit: interactive onboarding dashboard, test suite, seed data, API collection, and a 90-day launch playbook (full inventory). It does not automatically wire every third-party account for you — that keeps the entry price honest. Your bundle includes a practical activation guide if you want to connect tools yourself.

Foundation includes activation guides and .env.example so you can connect third-party services (payments, CRM, email) on your own timeline — or hand them to a developer.

Live operational connections (automated payment setup, CRM wiring, workflow automation) are coming soon in future tiers. See what's included for current status.

How does the multi-model viability analysis work?

Multiple independent evaluations score your idea across several business lenses, then reconcile into one clear verdict. If the story doesn’t hold together, that shows up — before you spend. A final independent stress test helps surface weak points before they cost you money.

What is the ownership model?

You own what we deliver in your repository: product code, documents, and configuration. What’s “live” in third-party dashboards depends on what you activate using the guides in your bundle. Your bundle includes activation guidance; use what’s included to see typical readiness by category. Legal and regulated use still requires your qualified advisors.

What input methods are supported?

Nine ways to describe your venture: type a description, speak via voice, paste a competitor URL for analysis, upload or photograph an image (business card, whiteboard, storefront), open your camera for live capture, record a video walkthrough, upload a document (pitch deck, business plan, research PDF), have a conversational interview with AI, or start from a template. All inputs are processed by vision-capable AI models to extract a structured business brief.

Do you support non‑US businesses or enterprise procurement?

Defaults (legal copy, entity examples, some vendor regions) are US-oriented. Non‑US and regulated industries need advisor review. Enterprise procurement (SSO, MSA, custom DPA) is not self-serve in the builder — contact sales for vendor review workflows.

What’s the difference between Strategy Pack and Foundation?

Strategy Pack ($697) generates all 90+ documents — strategy, legal, financial, marketing, and operations — as a downloadable .zip file. No code generation, no deployment. Perfect if you need the full planning + narrative pack but aren’t ready for a live product.

Foundation ($1,997) includes everything in Strategy Pack plus production code (Next.js app), deployment to your domain, backend API routes, design system, and a Day-1 Success Kit. If you start with Strategy Pack and upgrade to Foundation within 30 days, the $697 is credited toward the $1,997.
