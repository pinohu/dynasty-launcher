Frequently asked
Technical and commercial questions

What does a build deliver?

Depending on tier: a deployed production application with authenticated email, Stripe Connected Account for payment processing, CRM records, AI voice agent, email marketing sequences, SMS templates, 5 SEO articles, explainer video, brand assets, PostHog analytics, chatbot, 7 automation workflows, legal documents (ToS, Privacy Policy, Service Agreement), and a 15-question investor readiness assessment. All code is owned by you in a private GitHub repository.

Are these live service accounts or mockups?

Live accounts provisioned via API calls to production systems. Stripe Connected Accounts accept real payments immediately. Email campaigns are created and ready to activate. Automation workflows are deployed and active on n8n. Where an API limitation exists, OPERATIONS.md provides exact configuration steps.

What is the ownership model?

You own the code outright — pushed to your private GitHub repository. Infrastructure runs on your domain. Stripe billing operates through a Connected Account under Dynasty's platform (you receive a dashboard and full control). Clerk authentication uses Dynasty's managed instance with an isolated organization for your users. All credentials are delivered in CREDENTIALS.md.

How does the viability analysis work?

Up to 14 AI models analyze your business across 8 frameworks (Blue Ocean, Porter's 5 Forces, 7 Powers, JTBD, Lean Canvas, TAM/SAM/SOM, Unit Economics, Competitive Positioning) simultaneously. A weighted consensus determines the composite score. A Devil's Advocate model then stress-tests the highest-rated pivot. The result is a defensible viability assessment suitable for investor conversations.

What ongoing support is available?

Every build includes OPERATIONS.md with a complete business formation checklist and referrals to Dynasty Empire ecosystem partners: PA CROP Services for LLC formation and registered agent services, Notroom for document notarization, Neat Circle for ongoing business process automation, and CXReact for lead generation systems. Directory listing opportunities through erie.pro for applicable markets.

What platforms and deployment targets are supported?

Next.js on Vercel (SaaS, dashboards, AI platforms), WordPress on 20i (authority sites, local businesses), Brilliant Directories (membership and directory sites), static HTML on Vercel (landing pages and microsites), and SuiteDash (client portal and service businesses).
