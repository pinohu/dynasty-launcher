FAQ

Straight answers

What strategy documents are generated?

You receive 90+ artifacts (typically about 30,000–50,000 words of core material) spanning strategy, revenue and operating model, go-to-market, investor- and lender-ready packs, competitive landscape, pitch narrative, risk and failure-mode runbooks, product specification, data and API design, design system, content outlines, and customer discovery — organized in your bundle so your team (and your advisors) can work from one place.

Can I use these documents for funding applications?

Yes — the bundle is built to support fundraising and lending conversations with serious structure (plans, numbers, narrative, diligence-friendly organization). Treat them as a strong starting point: have your attorney, CPA, and any industry advisors review before you sign, file, or submit.

What live infrastructure is provisioned?

Foundation delivers your document set + live product + deployment. It does not automatically wire every third-party account for you — that keeps the entry price honest. Your bundle includes a practical activation guide if you want to connect tools yourself, or you can upgrade.

Professional adds the core live business stack where integrations succeed (domain/email patterns, payments, CRM, sequences, chat, analytics, automations — subject to approvals and what your build includes).

Enterprise attempts the broadest set (voice, SMS, deeper content and creative pushes, directory paths, and more) when your package includes them.

Some steps always need your sign-off (identity verification, campaign review). See what’s included for status by category.

How does the multi-model viability analysis work?

Multiple independent evaluations score your idea across several business lenses, then reconcile into one clear verdict. If the story doesn’t hold together, that shows up — before you spend. A final independent stress test helps surface weak points before they cost you money.

What is the ownership model?

You own what we deliver in your repository: product code, documents, and configuration. What’s “live” in third-party dashboards depends on what was connected during your build tier and session. Your bundle includes activation guidance; use what’s included to see typical readiness by category. Legal and regulated use still requires your qualified advisors.

What input methods are supported?

Nine ways to describe your venture: type a description, speak via voice, paste a competitor URL for analysis, upload or photograph an image (business card, whiteboard, storefront), open your camera for live capture, record a video walkthrough, upload a document (pitch deck, business plan, research PDF), have a conversational interview with AI, or start from a template. All inputs are processed by vision-capable AI models to extract a structured business brief.

Do you support non‑US businesses or enterprise procurement?

Defaults (legal copy, entity examples, some vendor regions) are US-oriented. Non‑US and regulated industries need advisor review. Enterprise procurement (SSO, MSA, custom DPA) is not self-serve in the builder — contact sales for vendor review workflows.
