How we validate your idea

Before we build anything, we pressure-test the business.

You get a viability score backed by multiple analytical frameworks and stress-tested by an adversarial model — so you know exactly where you stand before investing further.
🔬

Scored across 8 frameworks

Your business is analyzed through Blue Ocean Strategy, Porter's 5 Forces, Hamilton Helmer's 7 Powers, Jobs-to-be-Done, Lean Canvas, market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM), Unit Economics, and Competitive Positioning. You see exactly where your idea is strong and where it's vulnerable.

🤖

14 models vote — weighted consensus

Up to 14 AI models independently evaluate your business in parallel — then vote. Frontier models carry 2x weight, preventing any single model's blind spots from skewing the result. If consensus falls below 60%, models deliberate in additional rounds. You get a defensible composite score backed by the same analytical rigor investors expect.

😈

Adversarial red-teaming included

After consensus forms, an adversarial model attacks your strategy from every angle — finding weaknesses, hidden assumptions, competitive blind spots, and market risks. The full critique is included in your deliverables. Better to discover these in a document than in a pitch meeting or after you've spent money.

Frequently asked

Technical and commercial questions

What strategy documents are generated?

90+ documents totaling 30,000-50,000 words. Strategy documents: BUSINESS-SYSTEM.md (strategic architecture following MECE frameworks), REVENUE-MODEL.md (12-month bottoms-up financial projections with unit economics), GTM-PLAYBOOK.md (90-day launch sprint plan), INVESTOR-READINESS.md (VC due diligence + SBA loan eligibility + M&A exit package — all in one document), SBA-BUSINESS-PLAN.md (full SBA-format with 3-year financials), COMPETITIVE-ANALYSIS.md (real competitors with actual pricing), PITCH-DECK.md (10-slide investor pitch content), AGENT-SYSTEM.md (AI workforce architecture with 7 automation workflows), and FAILURE-MODES.md (10 failure scenarios with recovery runbooks). Technical documents: SPEC.md (product specification with acceptance criteria), DATA-MODEL.md + API-CONTRACTS.md (database schema and API endpoints), DESIGN.md (complete design system), KB-OUTLINES.md (10 SEO article outlines), and CUSTOMER-DISCOVERY.md (interview script with scoring rubric). Generated by 14 AI models with weighted consensus scoring across 8 frameworks.

Can I use these documents for funding applications?

Yes. The INVESTOR-READINESS.md addresses all 10 criteria VCs evaluate, all 10 SBA loan requirements, and all 10 elements Shark Tank investors assess. The SBA-BUSINESS-PLAN.md follows the exact format SBA loan officers require — including 3-year P&L, monthly cash flow projections, break-even analysis, and funding request with use-of-proceeds breakdown. The M&A section covers everything an acquirer needs: EBITDA, valuation multiples, customer concentration, IP portfolio, and integration considerations. These documents are generated by frontier AI models and should be reviewed by your advisors before submission.

What live infrastructure is provisioned?

Foundation ($1,997): generated application + documents in your repo and deployment — the server does not auto-run the integration module suite (no Stripe/CRM vendor hooks in provision_modules for that tier). Use OPERATIONS.md for manual setup or upgrade.

Professional: adds the core live stack where APIs succeed — e.g. domain/email patterns, connected payments, CRM, email sequences, chatbot, analytics, automation workflows (subject to keys and archetype deferrals).

Enterprise: broadest module set (voice, SMS, SEO content pushes, creative, directory/WP paths, etc.) when not skipped by your site package.

Some items always need owner activation (e.g. Stripe identity, campaign review). See what ships today for implementation status by category.

How does the multi-model viability analysis work?

Up to 14 frontier AI models analyze your business across 8 frameworks simultaneously. Each model works independently — preventing groupthink. A weighted consensus (frontier models count more) determines the composite viability score. If consensus falls below 60%, models deliberate in additional rounds. A Devil's Advocate model then attacks the winning strategy ruthlessly. The result survives adversarial stress-testing and is defensible in investor conversations.

What is the ownership model?

You own the generated outputs delivered in your repository: code, documents, and configs. Foundation: private repo + deployment artifacts; live payment dashboards and vendor CREDENTIALS.md entries appear when those services are actually provisioned (typically Professional+). Professional / Enterprise: connected accounts and third-party dashboards apply where integrations succeed — see what ships today. OPERATIONS.md covers formation and activation steps; advisor review still applies for legal and regulated use.

What input methods are supported?

Nine ways to describe your venture: type a description, speak via voice, paste a competitor URL for analysis, upload or photograph an image (business card, whiteboard, storefront), open your camera for live capture, record a video walkthrough, upload a document (pitch deck, business plan, research PDF), have a conversational interview with AI, or start from a template. All inputs are processed by vision-capable AI models to extract a structured business brief.

Do you support non‑US businesses or enterprise procurement?

Defaults (legal copy, entity examples, some vendor regions) are US-oriented. Non‑US and regulated industries need advisor review. Enterprise procurement (SSO, MSA, custom DPA) is not self-serve in the builder — contact sales for vendor review workflows.
